i am running into a new year

and the old years blow back

like a wind

that i catch in my hair

like strong fingers like

all my old promises and

it will be hard to let go

of what i said to myself

about myself

when i was sixteen and

twenty-six and thirty-six

even thirty-six but

i am running into a new year

and i beg what i love and

i leave to forgive me

How to add formatting to a word

Wrap the word in a <span> with a class:

<span class="squiggle">word</span>

Available classes:

  • squiggle — wavy underline
  • size-up — larger text
  • size-down — smaller text
  • above — lifted above the line
  • below — dropped below the line
  • tracking-up — letters farther apart
  • tracking-down — letters closer together

Classes can be combined: <span class="size-up tracking-up">word</span>
