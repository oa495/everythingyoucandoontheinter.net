Scroll Wheel

Use your scroll wheel or trackpad to scroll through this text.

The web was one of the first interfaces where scrolling became a primary mode of navigation — the infinite canvas, revealed by the wheel.

Before the scroll wheel was common, users clicked scrollbar arrows one notch at a time.

Today, trackpad gestures, touchscreen swipes, and the scroll wheel all map to the same underlying browser scroll event.

The scroll wheel also controls zoom in many applications (Cmd/Ctrl + scroll).
